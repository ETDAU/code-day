#codeday #ops

Digital Solutions Day (formerly known as Code Day) is a full day event where you can immerse yourself in learning or developing a digital tool, program, or language that will help you and your team.

No matter what you're working on, digital skills can make your job easier! Use Digital Solutions Day to take the day to learn and make something awesome with your fellow OPS colleagues.

Who is this event for?

  • You keep hearing the words digital literacy, big data, automation, agile and would like to know what it means.

  • Have been looking for a space to work on your digital, data or analytics project with like-minded peers?

  • You have a digital or data project you’ve always wanted to try, but haven’t had the opportunity? Or you are eager to see what innovative and exciting data projects are being undertaken elsewhere in the OPS?

  • If you relate to any of these, then this event is for you!

    • What if I'm not a coder or data analyst?

    This event is open to OPS employees of any and all experience levels. You do not need to be a coder to attend. If you work with data or digital tools in any capacity, you are more than welcome to attend!

    There is something we want to communicate to you. You can learn how to do awesome digital things
